Frequently asked questions

What kind of queries we can ask on Issue Tracker? +

You can ask any queries that are realted to IITGn. For example if you have academic related queries then it will be entertained.

How much time does it takes to get my query resolved? +

It can take from 1 day to at max 7 days.If your query is not resolved within 7 days fill the query again.

Who are the Admins? +

We have opted the students from IITGn who have CPI greater than 8.0 can register them self as Admin.

How can I Sign Up? +

You can simply click on Registration from the Navbar. After than you can fill the relevant details. Make sure you fill the details carefully.

If you have specific query then please fill the Query form by logging in the portal.
